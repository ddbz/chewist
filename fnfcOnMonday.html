Friday Night Fried Chicken on Monday

by Jordan Diez

Katie's Friday is on Monday. We've got a couple days off.

So we decided to watch fights, and celebrate a full happy home.

I threw out the idea of fried chicken, but healthier. Old Bay sweet potato crusted chicken thighs. Sounds good right? Maybe some braised collared greens with a reduced balsamic. That is a dish totally within our capability right now. A good cast iron pan. A gas stove.

But we've all had some long days. It's been a long week. But they've been good days. Great weeks.

Instead Katie made chocolate chip cookies. They were amazing. Hot and chewy... so good... Then grilled cheese, with.... American!, avocado, and bacon jam. The side: half sour pickle and chips.

That meal. And it is a meal, hits some nostalgic notes especially hard for me.

And the fact is, some days, you've earned what you crave.

Here at the Chewist, we call that:

Friday Night Fried Chicken... on Monday

except we didn't have fried chicken...

And we follow it up with a nice tall glass of Maker's Mark, on ice. We watch fights and they are EPIC!

Here at the Chewist, we rock our Fridays on Mondays.

And one of these Mondays, we might even have chicken.
